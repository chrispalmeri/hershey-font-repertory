Editing

Click a path segment to show its nodes. Then click and drag a node to move it.

Your delete key will delete a node or an entire segment, depending on which is selected.

Double click on a selected line segment to add a node.

There isn't a convenient way to add new segments. You would have to add them in the Path textarea as SVG commands.
